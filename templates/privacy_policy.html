Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025 | Last Updated: September 22, 2025

Your Privacy is Our Priority

VB Healthcare Management System is committed to protecting your health information and maintaining the highest standards of privacy and security.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Health Information (PHI)
  • Medical history, diagnoses, and treatment records
  • Prescription and medication information
  • Laboratory test results and imaging reports
  • Appointment schedules and consultation notes
  • Insurance and billing information
Personal Information
  • Name, date of birth, and contact information
  • Emergency contact details
  • Employment and demographic information
  • Authentication credentials and security questions
Technical Information
  • IP addresses and device information
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Usage patterns and system performance data
  • Security logs and access records

2. How We Use Your Information

Treatment & Care
  • Providing medical services
  • Coordinating care between providers
  • Managing appointments and schedules
  • Sending appointment reminders
Healthcare Operations
  • Quality assurance and improvement
  • Staff training and development
  • System maintenance and security
  • Compliance monitoring

3. Information Sharing & Disclosure

Limited Disclosure Policy

We only share your information when necessary for your care, as required by law, or with your explicit written consent.

Authorized Disclosures
  • Healthcare Providers: For coordinated patient care
  • Insurance Companies: For billing and coverage verification
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or court order
  • Public Health: For disease prevention and health monitoring
  • Emergency Situations: To protect life or prevent serious harm

4. Data Security Measures

Encryption
  • 256-bit AES encryption at rest
  • TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  • End-to-end encryption for communications
Access Controls
  • Multi-factor authentication
  • Role-based access permissions
  • Regular access audits
Monitoring
  • 24/7 security monitoring
  • Intrusion detection systems
  • Automated threat response
Infrastructure
  • HIPAA-compliant cloud hosting
  • Redundant data backups
  • Disaster recovery protocols

5. Your Rights

Access & Portability
  • Request copies of your records
  • Export your data in standard formats
  • View access logs of your information
Control & Correction
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Restrict certain uses of your information
  • Request deletion where legally permitted

6. Compliance & Regulations

HIPAA

Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act

GDPR

General Data Protection Regulation

HITECH

Health Information Technology for Economic and Clinical Health Act
