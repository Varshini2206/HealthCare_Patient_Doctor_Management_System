Our Story

Founded in 2020 by visionary technologist Venkat Buthuru, VB Healthcare was born from a simple yet powerful idea: healthcare should be accessible, efficient, and patient-centered.

After witnessing firsthand the challenges patients face in managing their healthcare journey, our founder assembled a world-class team of healthcare professionals, software engineers, and user experience designers to create a solution that truly serves both patients and providers.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of patients and work with hundreds of healthcare professionals, always staying true to our core mission of making healthcare better for everyone.
